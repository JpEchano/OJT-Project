SUGARCANE is a six-piece folk-pop band based in Manila. The band started by doing covers but has since
transitioned into creating their unique sound, becoming known for their distinctive music and heartfelt storytelling.
Their musical compositions are imbued with the core elements of folk-pop, blending sweet melodies with
meaningful lyrics that take listeners on a journey of emotions. And now, as part of Warner Music Philippines'
roster of talented artists, SUGARCANE is poised to create even greater music that will further showcase
their artistry and passion for their craft. The band is looking forward to continuing to create more impactful music
that will inspire and touch the hearts of people around the world.
